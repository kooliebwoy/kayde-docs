# Quick Start Guide

Get your first Kayde website live in under 5 minutes. This guide will walk you through creating an account, building your first site, and publishing it to the web.

## Prerequisites

- A modern web browser
- Email address for account creation
- Basic understanding of web concepts (helpful but not required)

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. **Sign up at [builder.kayde.io](https://builder.kayde.io)**
   ```
   https://builder.kayde.io/app/connect
   ```

2. **Verify your email** - Check your inbox and click the verification link

3. **Complete your profile** - Add your name and choose a username

## Step 2: Create Your First Site

1. **Click "New Site"** from your dashboard
2. **Choose a template** or start from scratch
3. **Select your site type:**
   - **Landing Page** - Perfect for businesses and portfolios
   - **Blog** - Content-focused sites with built-in CMS
   - **E-commerce** - Online stores with payment integration
   - **Custom** - Build anything with our visual editor

- **Hero Sections** - Eye-catching headers with CTAs
- **Feature Grids** - Showcase your products or services
- **Contact Forms** - Collect leads and customer inquiries  
- **Galleries** - Display images and portfolios
- **Testimonials** - Build trust with customer reviews

## Step 4: Add Content

1. **Edit text** by clicking directly on any text element
2. **Upload images** using the media manager
3. **Create pages** from the Pages panel
4. **Set up navigation** in the site settings

### Content Management

```javascript
// Example: Adding dynamic content via API
const response = await fetch('/api/content', {
  method: 'POST',
  headers: {
    'Authorization': 'Bearer your-api-key',
    'Content-Type': 'application/json'
  },
  body: JSON.stringify({
    title: 'Welcome to Kayde',
    content: 'Your website content here...',
    status: 'published'
  })

```

## Step 5: Configure Settings

### Site Settings
- **SEO** - Meta titles, descriptions, and social sharing
- **Analytics** - Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel
- **Forms** - Email notifications and integrations
- **Performance** - Image optimization and caching

### Domain Setup
1. Choose a free `.kayde.io` subdomain, or
2. Connect your custom domain (`yoursite.com`)
3. SSL certificates are automatically provisioned

## Step 6: Publish Your Site

1. **Preview your site** using the preview button
2. **Check for errors** in the validation panel  
3. **Click "Publish"** to make your site live
4. **Share your URL** - your site is now accessible worldwide
